My name is Jennifer Lu and I am a 20-year-old undergraduate student with a major in Biology. I am currently attending the University of California, Riverside, and planning to graduate in June 2024. I am on the pre-med track hoping to become a surgeon one day. Becoming part of the medical field has always been my dream since I was a little child. I find it super interesting to learn about all the different types of medicine and cures with the help of advanced technology. Although I am not super certain of what I want to become in the future, the pre-med track is what I have been striving for since the start of college. I hope that I can get into medical school after I graduate in about two years but I know that it is very difficult and I have been stressing over it. I always wonder what would happen if I chose to switch to a different major or do something else instead and what my life would be with a little less stress. However, this is already a little too late for me since I have a plan for my future career and have no desire to switch it anytime soon at least.
